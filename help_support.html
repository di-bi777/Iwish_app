Help & Support

Welcome to the Help & Support page. Here you can find answers to common questions and ways to contact us for further assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I add an item to my wishlist?
    Tap the "+" button on the home screen and fill in the item details.
  • How do I edit or delete an item?
    Tap on an item in your wishlist, then use the edit or delete options.
  • How is my data stored?
    Your data is securely stored in the cloud and is only accessible by you.
  • How do I change my account settings?
    Go to the Settings screen and select the option you want to update.

Contact Support

If you need further assistance, please contact us at support@yourdomain.com. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

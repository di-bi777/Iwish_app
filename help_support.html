Welcome to the Help & Support page. Here you can find answers to common questions and ways to contact us for further assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I add an item to my wishlist?
    Tap the "+" button on the home screen, then enter the item details to add it to your wishlist.
  • How do I edit or delete an item?
    On the home screen, tap the edit button after selecting the item you wish to modify or remove.
  • How can I delete the data of an item after it's been achieved?
    Please swipe the item card to the left in the Achievements section of the Analytics screen to delete it.
  • How is my data stored?
    Your data is securely stored in the cloud and can only be accessed by you.
  • How do I change my account settings?
    Please go to the Settings screen and select the option you would like to update.
